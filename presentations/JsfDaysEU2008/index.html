II. What We Heard

After taking the steps to listen to what should be in JSF 2.0, here's what we heard and how we prioritized it.

Details 

  A. Top Five Goals for Early Draft Review
     1. Make custom components much easier to develop (EZComp)
     2. Ajax support
     3. Page description language (PDL)
     4. Reduce the configuration burden
     5. Provide for better compatability between JSF component libraries
        from different vendors

   B. Other really important things
     1. State management rewrite
     2. Bookmarkable URLs
     3. Zero deployment time
     4. Tree traversal
     5. Scopes
     6. Extension prioritization scheme
     7. Better error reporting

III. What We Are Doing About It

After prioritizing the requirements, we started satisfying them.

Details 

  A. History
    1. Started formally gathering requirements in October 2006
